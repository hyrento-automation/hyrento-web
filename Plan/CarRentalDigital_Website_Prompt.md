## PROJECT OVERVIEW

**Brand:** CarRental.digital  
**Tagline:** The Complete Operating System for Car Rental Businesses  
**Target Market:** US & European car rental businesses — small operators (20–50 fleet) to large multi-brand enterprises (500+ fleet)  
**Tone:** Professional, confident, clean, modern — like a premium B2B SaaS product  
**Primary Goal:** Convert car rental business owners into paying subscribers by making it crystal clear what problem is solved and how the system works — from day one to scale.
